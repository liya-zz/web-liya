I’m a forward-thinking product designer with a passion for transforming complexity into clarity. I specialize in turning ambitious ideas into impactful digital experiences through deep exploration and practical innovation.

Work That Speaks

GoTogether – Redefining Group Travel Planning

I led the end-to-end design of a collaborative travel planning app that simplifies group decision-making through intuitive UX and delightful UI.

From user research and wireframes to interactive visual design, we solved the chaotic group decision-making process through well-timed notifications, shared boards, and simplified itinerary tools. GoTogether became the go-to platform for collaborative trip planning from ideation to execution.

CourseRoll: Reimagining Course Enrollment for NYU IDM Students

To simplify course selection, I crafted this cohesive UX to align with student habits. The design eliminated confusion, boosted confidence, and improved overall satisfaction.

After user interviews, pain point analysis, and visual audits, we used Apple’s system design principles. Users are now able to compare courses, track credit needs, and find ideal class paths faster, by highlighting the precise structure and next steps.
